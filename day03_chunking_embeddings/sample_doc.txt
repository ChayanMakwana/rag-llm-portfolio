Welcome to Acme Corp!

We’re excited to have you on board. This document outlines key information about our company culture, leave policies, benefits, and employee responsibilities.

---

Company Culture

At Acme Corp, we value innovation, collaboration, and continuous learning. We encourage open communication and diversity in all forms. Every employee is empowered to contribute ideas and make a difference.

---

Working Hours and Flexibility

Our standard workweek is 40 hours, Monday through Friday. Core hours are between 10 AM to 4 PM. Employees may choose flexible schedules with manager approval.

Remote work is supported across all departments, subject to team needs.

---

Leave Policy

All full-time employees are entitled to 18 days of paid annual leave, 12 days of sick leave, and 10 public holidays per year.

Leave requests should be submitted at least 5 business days in advance, unless it's an emergency.

Unused annual leave can be carried forward for up to 2 years.

---

Health and Wellness Benefits

We offer comprehensive health insurance including dental and vision. All employees also receive access to a fitness reimbursement program and free counseling sessions.

---

Security and Code of Conduct

Employees must follow our IT security policy, including use of VPNs for remote access and two-factor authentication.

Any form of discrimination, harassment, or data misuse will result in disciplinary action.

---

Need Help?

Reach out to HR or your onboarding buddy with any questions. We’re here to help you succeed!
